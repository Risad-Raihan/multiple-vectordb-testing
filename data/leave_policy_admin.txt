=== ACCESS: admin ===
This document provides admin-level access to leave-related analytics and controls.

1. Historical Leave Data:
HR has access to leave patterns and usage by department to identify trends and operational risks.

2. Approver Controls:
Admins can designate leave approvers and escalation protocols in the HR system.

3. Documentation:
All leave requests and medical certificates must be digitally archived for 3 years.

4. Policy Exceptions:
Only HR managers can authorize exceptions to standard leave policies in documented special cases.
